About Latino Travel Agency

Founded in 2010, Latino Travel Agency has grown from a small local tour operator to Myanmar's leading travel company. What began as a passion project of three friends who wanted to share the beauty of their homeland has now become a trusted name in travel experiences.

We specialize in creating authentic, immersive travel experiences that showcase the rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality of Myanmar. Our team of local experts handcrafts each itinerary to ensure you discover the true essence of this magical country.

Our Mission

To connect travelers with the heart and soul of Myanmar through sustainable, ethical tourism practices that benefit local communities while preserving our cultural heritage.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most responsible and innovative travel company in Southeast Asia, setting new standards for authentic cultural experiences.
